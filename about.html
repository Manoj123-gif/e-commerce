About Us

Welcom To Phlox

At Phlox, we’re passionate about all things tech. Our mission is to bring you the latest and most innovative gadgets that enhance your daily life. Whether you’re a tech enthusiast, a busy professional, or someone who simply loves cool gadgets, we’ve got something for you.

Our Story

Our journey began in a small garage, fueled by curiosity and a desire to make technology accessible to everyone. As gadget enthusiasts ourselves, we saw a gap in the market for high-quality, affordable tech products. And so, Phlox was born.

Meet Our Team


1. Manoj Shrestha (Founder & CEO):

A self-proclaimed gadget geek, Manoj has always been fascinated by the intersection of design and functionality. With a background in engineering, he’s the driving force behind our product selection.

2. Emily Chen (Chief Product Officer):

Emily’s eye for detail ensures that every gadget we offer meets our strict quality standards. She’s the one who tirelessly tests and evaluates each product before it lands on our shelves.

3. Mark Reynolds (Customer Experience Guru):

is the friendly voice behind our customer support. He’s committed to making your experience with Gadget Haven seamless and delightful.

Our Values


1. Innovation:

Constantly on the lookout for groundbreaking gadgets that push the boundaries of what’s possible.

2. Quality:

We believe that every gadget should be built to last. That’s why we curate products from reputable manufacturers.

3. Customer-Centric Approach:

Your satisfaction is our top priority. We’re here to assist you at every step, from choosing the right gadget to troubleshooting any issues.

Why Choose Us?


1. Curated Selection:

We handpick gadgets that combine style, functionality, and affordability.

2. Expert Advice:

Have questions? Our team is here to guide you toward the perfect gadget for your needs.

3. Secure Shopping:

Your data security matters to us. We use the latest encryption technology to protect your information.

Join the Gadget Revolution

Explore our collection of cutting-edge gadgets and discover how technology can enhance your life. From smart home devices to portable chargers, we’ve got it all.

Thank you for being part of the Gadget Haven family!
